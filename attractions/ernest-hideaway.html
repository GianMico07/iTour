Ernest Hideaway

Release Date: October 25, 2022
Location: Purok 3, Brgy. San Lorenzo

- °C

-

Details

San Pablo, Laguna is home to seven famous lakes, so finding a lakeside resort or stay isn’t new. This includes Ernest Hideaway, which covers an expansive garden with various outdoor amenities and a cozy Fil-Am-inspired cabin. It’s a well-beloved place for simple staycations, day tours with the family, prenup shoots, and even team buildings for companies.


One thing to consider is that the property is uphill, and you will be going up and down stairs to have access to its various amenities. Take note of this especially if you’re staying with the elderly! Other than that, the property is one dreamy place to make new memories with loved ones.


Your group will have access to the whole place exclusively, which is perfect now that the holidays are around the corner. If you’re planning a reunion with your angkan, Ernest Hideaway can accommodate up to 30 guests.


The main accommodation is located at the top of the hill; a cozy cabin with multiple rooms and a very spacious function hall. In the backyard, there’s a dreamy seating arrangement in the garden, which is illuminated by charming fairy lights at night.


Ernest Hideaway is located at Purok 3 Brgy. San Lorenzo, San Pablo City, Laguna. To get there, enter “Ernest Hideaway” on Waze or Google Maps. Rates depend on the number of guests and rooms where you want to stay. Overnight rates start at P15,000 per night, including four air-conditioned rooms, a standard room, a family room, two kubos, and exclusive access to the property’s amenities. To book a stay, send a message on Facebook or call 0917 513 1539.
