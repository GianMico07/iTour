Details

Sitio de Felicidad is a vibrant and lively outdoor food park located in Brgy. Sto. Angel, San Pablo City. Set in an alfresco setting, this food park offers a wide variety of delicious and diverse food options that are sure to please any palate. The ambiance at Sitio de Felicidad is warm and welcoming, with a laid-back and relaxed atmosphere that is perfect for casual get-togethers with friends and family. Whether you're looking to grab a quick bite to eat, enjoy a leisurely meal, or just sit back and take in the sights and sounds of the bustling food park, Sitio de Felicidad has something for everyone.
